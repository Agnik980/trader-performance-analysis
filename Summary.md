Strategy 1: 
Risk Control in Fear Markets
During Fear periods, traders tend to have lower PnL and higher losses. It is recommended to reduce leverage and trade with smaller position sizes to minimize risk.

Strategy 2: 
Avoid Overtrading in Greed Markets
In Greed conditions, traders increase trade frequency and leverage. Traders should avoid overtrading and consider booking profits early to protect gains.

**Strategy 3: 
Segment-Based Strategy **
High leverage traders show higher volatility in returns. It is advisable for high leverage traders to trade selectively during stable (Greed) periods and avoid Fear phases.
